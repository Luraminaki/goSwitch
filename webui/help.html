How to Play

Every square is either lit or dark. Clicking a square switches it -- and, depending on the pattern below, its neighbors too. The goal: get every square on the board into the same state, all lit or all dark.

Toggle Pattern

Grid Configuration controls which cells a click actually flips:

  • 0 -- the clicked square itself.
  • 4 -- its up/down/left/right neighbors.
  • 8 -- its diagonal neighbors.

Any combination can be active at once. Change the grid size or pattern, then Reset (with config) to deal a new board.

Undo & Move History

Game Trivia lists every square you've switched, in order. Switching the same square twice cancels itself back out, so it drops off the list if nothing else has changed since. Undo reverts whichever move is now last on that list.

Cheat

Turning on Enable Cheat reveals one sequence of squares under Winning Combination that solves the current board -- there's often more than one.

Sessions

The Sessions badge shows how many players are active out of the server's capacity. If it's full, you'll wait in line and join automatically once a slot frees up -- no need to refresh.
